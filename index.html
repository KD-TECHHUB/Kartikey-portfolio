About me

Tech brain,
supply chain heart

I spent nearly three years at ADP building and maintaining cloud infrastructure: CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes clusters, Splunk dashboards. It gave me a precision-first mindset that most supply chain professionals don't come with.

Before that, I was hands-on in my family's construction business: chasing deliveries, managing suppliers, coordinating labour on-site. That's where I got the supply chain bug. Real problems, real stakes, no textbook answers.

Now I'm at the University of Birmingham, finishing an MSc in Supply Chain Management while working with the UK Department for Transport on a live consultancy project, developing strategies to get SMEs and VCSEs into public sector supply chains.

I'm looking for roles where both sides of me are useful: the engineer who can read a dataset and the operator who can fix a broken process.
